BREAKING CRYSTAL BLACKJACK

Heisenberg will give you the number of crystals he needs for a batch of blue ice at the start of the game.

There are four types of crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number. You lose the game if your total score surpasses the random number.

Produce the exact amount before delivery!

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Every moment the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.
